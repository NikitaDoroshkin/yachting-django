About Burlington Harbor Marina

Burlington Harbor Marina is designed to provide the finest facilities to satisfy all of your boating needs.

We are located on spectacular Lake Champlain with outstanding access to parks, recreation, and amenities including downtown Burlington’s Church Street market place, restaurants, shopping, bike paths, walking trails, breweries, entertainment, and more.

The number one destination on the lake is now available for transient and seasonal boaters in an exceptional marine environment.
